HOW TO PLAY

KEYBOARD CONTROLS

  • ← / A  Move left
  • → / D  Move right
  • ↑ / ↓  Move up / down a lane
  • Space  Activate Solidarity (when chain is ready & near a barrier)
  • Escape  Pause / unpause
  • R / Enter  Restart after game over

TOUCHSCREEN CONTROLS

  • Swipe left / right  Move left / right
  • Swipe up / down  Move up / down a lane
  • Tap  Activate Solidarity (when chain is ready & near a barrier)
  • Tap after game over  Restart (brief cooldown prevents accidental restart)

OBJECTIVE

  • Collect all 8 HTG members to build your chain.
  • Activate Solidarity to clear barriers (precedents). Clear 4 to win.
  • Avoid suits (break your chain), cabinets (slow you down), and bots (lock a lane).
