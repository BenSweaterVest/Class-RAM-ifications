# Mechanics Guide

Last updated: March 22, 2026

## Objective

Clear 4 barriers by activating Solidarity at the right moment while meeting rising phase thresholds.

## Phase Thresholds

- Clear 1 requires chain 3
- Clear 2 requires chain 5
- Clear 3 requires chain 7
- Clear 4 requires chain 9

## Controls

- Left/A: move left
- Right/D: move right
- Up/Down: lane change
- Space: attempt Solidarity
- R: restart

## Obstacles

- Suit: higher chain attrition pressure
- Cabinet: temporary movement slow
- Bot: temporary lane lock

## Solidarity Rules

Ready only when:
- run active
- barrier exists and is in range
- current phase threshold met

On success:
- temporary shield
- chain spent
- barrier clear increments precedent
- narrative checkpoint may trigger

## Narrative Checkpoints (5)

1. 1984 intro context card
2. Phase 1 clear card (hopeful)
3. Phase 2 clear card (happy)
4. Phase 3 clear card (somber)
5. Phase 4 victory card (EO 12968)

## Visual/Tone Layer

- Popup themes apply per checkpoint tone.
- Late-game status banner indicates Washington DC finale segment.
- Phase background keys are selected by precedent state with safe fallback.

## Win/Lose

Win:
- 4 successful clears and final victory card resolution.

Lose:
- unshielded barrier collision
- timeout before required clears
