# Historical Background

This game is inspired by *High Tech Gays v. Defense Industrial Security Clearance Office*.

## Key Parties

- High Tech Gays: a Silicon Valley social and advocacy organization for gay and lesbian tech workers, founded in 1983 in San Jose, California
- DISCO: the Defense Industrial Security Clearance Office, the Department of Defense office responsible for industrial security clearances

## Core Conflict

DISCO policy treated homosexuality as a security risk and subjected gay applicants to harsher scrutiny, extended investigations, and denial patterns tied to clearance eligibility.

## Timeline

- 1984: High Tech Gays filed suit in federal district court
- August 1987: the district court ruled for the plaintiffs
- February 2, 1990: the Ninth Circuit reversed that decision
- August 2, 1995: Executive Order 12968 barred discrimination in security-clearance decisions on the basis of sexual orientation

## Why It Matters To The Game

The game's stage structure mirrors this arc:

- early momentum and resistance-building
- a sharp reversal in 1990
- survival until policy change in 1995
